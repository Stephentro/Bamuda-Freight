Our Background Information

We are a premier provider of clearing and forwarding services, dedicated to facilitating seamless logistics and supply chain management for our esteemed clients. Located in the commercial capital of Nairobi which is a major hub for imports into Kenya and Congo, Malawi, Zambia and Uganda. With a robust understanding of the complexities of international trade, we pride ourselves on providing tailored solutions that meet the diverse needs of businesses across various sectors.

We offer a broad portfolio of services including Customs Clearance Services, Air Freight Services, Railways Transportation Services, Roadways Cargo Services, Sea Cargo Services, air freight logistics, Customs Clearance Services, Port Handling Services. Our expertise also includes providing a comprehensive and tailor-made solution to meet the relocation needs of corporate and residential clients of neighboring landlocked countries like Rwanda, Burundi, DR.

We strive to exceed customers expactation through professionalism, intergrity and a commitment to excellence through offering reliable, efficient and cost- effective clearing and forwarding services as our mission is to simplify the logistic process for our clients by offering reliable, efficient and cost-effective clearing and forwarding services

We navigate the intricacies of customs regulations, ensuring compliance and timely clearance of goods at ports of entry.Our experienced team coordinates and manages the shipment of goods from origin to destination, optimizing routes and minimizing costs as we offer end-to-end logistics solutions, including warehousing, inventory management, and distribution services tailored to our clients' unique requirements.

Our expertise in handling all necessary documentation ensures a smooth transaction process, reducing delays and enhancing efficiency.We provide strategic advice on trade regulations, logistics optimization, and supply chain management to help businesses navigate the complexities of international trade.
